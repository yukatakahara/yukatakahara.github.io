Frequently Asked Question



How well does this product with stand car washes?


We advise you not to go through a high pressure wash. As long as you are careful, it should last a very long time.




Will this affect my paint on my car?


As long as your car has good paint (manufacture paint), this will not hurt your car. We do not advise you adhere our product on cars that have recently been painted with nonmanufactured paint.




How easy is it to install?


Very simple! Most can do it alone but it's always nice to have an extra set of eyes and hands. The transfer paper that is attached to your art will allow you to easily adhere the art precisely and with no bubbling. Just maintain a steady hand and work slowly to remove all any bubbling.




How long will it take for my order to arrive?


We process your order immediately and is ready to ship with in 24 hours. Delivery through USPS can take anywhere from 1-5 business days.




Can I send you my own designs?


As long as your design is in vector format, email it to carfacial@gmail.com and we'll let you know. Most of the time we can certainly make it for you!
