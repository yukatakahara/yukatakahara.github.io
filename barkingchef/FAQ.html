Does Barking Chef's Homemade Dog Food follow AAFCO standards?

AAFCO standards are misleading and wrong when they suggest that the standard pet food can fill a pet's dietary needs. There is no such thing as a meal providing "complete and balanced nutrition," for both pets and for humans. AAFCO is just a guideline that pet industries must follow. We at Barking Chef have spent countless hours with hollistic veterinarians and pet nurtionists to come up with our recipe. We assure you that it is healthy! How can you compare 'real' food to kibble?!

What is the best way to transition my pet to Barking Chef food?

If your dog has a sensitive stomach it is best to start gradually. Give 1/2 of your current food and 1/2 of our food for about 2-4 days depending on the sensitivity of your dog. Day 5-7 give your dog 1/4 of your current food and 3/4 of our food. By day 7 your dog should be good to go! It is normal for a dog with a sensitive stomach to have a little bit of diarrhea on the first day or two.
