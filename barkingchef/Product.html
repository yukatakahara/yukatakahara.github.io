"So Natural . . . So Yummy!"



Most of us today barely have time to cook our own meals, let alone cook our dogs a meal everyday.

Barking Chef will cook and deliver homemade meals to your door step, your dog won't even have to know that you didnt make it yourself!  Our doggy meals are never frozen and made to order everytime.


Pricing Chart

Just like humans, dogs too get tired of eating the same meal over and over so we have provided 2-3 healthy meals.


We aim for variety because it helps to ensure the best balance of nutrients.

We don't use any specific supplements or artificial vitamins. We simply get all the nutrients we need from whole foods such as: fresh meats, vegetables, fruits, beans, and herbs.


We pride ourselves in using organic ingredients - free of chemicals.

We try our best to be as green as we can to help our environment. All of our meals are packaged in reused containers.


We encourage customers to return the used containers.
