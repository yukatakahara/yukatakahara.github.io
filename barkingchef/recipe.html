Recipe



Each dog is different in his or her likes and dislikes. It's a lot of work but we work hard to make sure that all of our customers receives a well balanced and delicious meal!


Here is one of Guinness's favorite dishes!



Guinness's chicken dance meal:

  • Raw Free Range Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices: Oregano, Cumin, Basil, Parsley, Thyme
  • glove of garlic
  • Vegetables: Cabbage, Collard Greens, Spinach, Carrots, Celery
  • Whole Wheat Pasta or rice
  • Ground egg shells (optional)
  • Apples (optional)


I take 2 raw free range chicken and put it in large pot with olive oil. I throw in a small crushed up garlic (fleas hate the smell and taste of garlic!), pinch of salt and pepper, and all kinds of herbs. Basil, Oregano, Thyme, Parsley, Cumin, cylantro.

Flip the chickens over so it browns a bit on each side. While thats browning, I cut up different types of fresh and frozen vegetables like celery, carrots, brocolli, cabbage, collard greens, and green beans. (She's not a fan of veggies but with the flavors of the chicken and herbs, she could care less whats in it!) We try to make sure we get as many colors of the rainbow goes in to her veggies!

We then add hot water to cover the chicken and let it simmer on low heat for 1-1.5 hours.

Then I take the chickens out and put in bags of organic whole wheat pasta. I fill it a little more with hot water and let it boil.
Meanwhile, I will have removed the chicken meat from the carcass and added it back into the mixture. Mix it all together and serve.

This lasts me exactly 7 days. I keep it in a air tight container in the fridge and we feed her morning and night.
