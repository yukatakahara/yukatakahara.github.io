Welcome to Barking Chef



If your dog could talk he would ask you, "why do you feed me this stale kibble every day?" "How come I can't eat 'real' food just like you?"


All dogs deserve to eat homemade meals everyday. Our doggy meals are made with 100% human ingredients and it's organic so it's guaranteed to be healthy.


We at Barking Chef want to encourage pet owners to understand the terrible ingredients inside dog food and dog treats. Even if you don't order from us, you should know what you are feeding your pets and make a change.
