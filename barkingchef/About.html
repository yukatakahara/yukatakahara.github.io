About us



Just like most of you, we beleived that our dog Guinness should only eat kibble and canned dog food every single day of her life.



Slowly we began to research more about animal nutrition and about the foods the pet food industry calls "nutritious". After years of researching and consulting with animal nutritionist and veterinarians, we created our very own recipe for Guinness.



After only a few months, friends and neighbors began to ask us for samples of Guinness's food and all have been thrilled with the results. Most importantly they are confident that their 4 legged family member is getting the proper nutrition and joy out of eating something that taste great.


We at Barking Chef hope to encourage and educate all pet owners to promote health and longevity to our loyal and deserving canine friends.
